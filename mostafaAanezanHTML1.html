Programming for A Better Future

Our mission is to use coding and digital skills to enable inclusion and employment, and as a catalyst for social innovation and diversity.

We are a combination between a coding bootcamp and a leadership course,
based in Beirut. Our aim is to get marginalised youth to participate in the growing needs of the local ICT and software sector.
